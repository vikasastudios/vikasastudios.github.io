Venture With Us!

Specializations

We specialize in the following services...

Technical Consulting and Analysis

For immediate or breaking challenges, we address specific issues related to data analysis, data management, and high performance IT systems. We take an objective and systematic approach to solving complex problems, bringing interdisciplinary expertise in data science, engineering, physics, and business analytics.

Policy Advisory and Risk Analytics

We apply a systems thinking approach to analysis, and deploy cross-disciplinary methodologies as appropriate to the subject area and available data. Recent application areas include spacecraft systems, market risk, real estate contracts, and space-based humanitarian relief.

Data Sensing-as-a-Service

We work with firms to analyze on-going streams of data generated as a result of normal operations. We conduct a comprehensive initial assessment to convert data gathering into real-time insights, and then develop analytics tools to generate and evolve this information monthly. This allows firms to react quickly to changes in customer behavior, market in dynamic systems, and eliminate emerging or unnecessary costs.

About

Vikasa is a Sanskrit word meaning expanding and developing, like the bloom of a flower when the sun rises on a calm and serene morning. And as it's meaning suggests, Vikasa Studios is a venture studio and think tank constantly focused on developing, growing and expanding our understanding of emerging sciences and technologies.



Vision

Our vision is to serve organisations (MNCs, NGOs, NPOs) and startups by venturing into unknown and emerging ideas by advising and advocacy.



Mission

We perform this with enterprising people where we ideate, analyse, build, test and repeat until our service is fulfilled to our customer.



Discipline

We specialize in Technical Consultancy and Analysis, Policy Advisory and Risk Analyses and; perform Data Sensing as a service.


Value and Culture

In a chaotic, noisy and khronos world, we actively seek the facts and practice strongly conscious capitalism and servant leadership. We believe the endurance of humanity is very important in this delicate world and as such diversity in all forms is at our core.
